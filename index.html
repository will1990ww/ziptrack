Comprehensive Q&A

Q: Do I need MCST approval to install outdoor blinds in my condo?
A: Yes, most condominiums require MCST approval. We make this entirely hassle-free for you by providing all necessary technical schematics, fabric specifications (e.g., 1% openness factor), and official color codes tailored to your condo’s specific guidelines to ensure 100% compliance and swift approval.
Q: Are the blinds fully rainproof and windproof?
A: Absolutely. Our systems are engineered to block heavy tropical rain and withstand strong winds, effectively turning your balcony into a dry, usable indoor extension. However, for extreme storm conditions, we recommend partially or fully retracting them to prolong the system's lifespan.
Q: How long does the measurement and installation process take?
A: After your confirmation, our professional in-house team will fabricate your customized blinds directly in our factory. The actual physical setup at your property is highly efficient and typically completed within a 2-Days Setup timeframe, leaving your home clean and ready to enjoy.
Q: What does the 5-Year Warranty cover?
A: We offer a robust 5-year warranty that fully covers both the Dooya electrical tubular motor and the premium fabric against manufacturing defects. This comprehensive protection is designed to give our clients and sellers maximum confidence in the longevity of our products.
Q: How do I clean and maintain the fabrics?
A: Maintenance is extremely low. You simply need to wipe the fabric with a soft, damp cloth or sponge using mild soapy water. Thanks to the Microban® antimicrobial technology in our Phifer fabrics, mold and mildew growth are naturally prevented.
