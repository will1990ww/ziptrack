❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need MCST approval to install outdoor blinds in my condo?

A: Yes. We make this easy by providing all necessary fabric specifications (e.g., 1% openness factor) and official color codes tailored to your condo’s guidelines to ensure smooth approval.

Q: Are your outdoor blinds fully waterproof and windproof?

A: Our Ziptrak® systems are highly weather-resistant. They effectively block heavy rain and withstand strong winds. For safety during extreme storms, we recommend retracting them.

Q: How long does the whole process take?

A: After a free site measurement and order confirmation, the actual installation in your home usually finishes within just one day.
