Phifer USA — The Industry Standard for Performance & Health
Imported directly from the USA, Phifer is the gold standard for outdoor balcony blinds in Singapore's tropical climate. Engineered with advanced fiberglass and polyester yarns, it delivers unmatched weather resistance and outward visibility.
  • Microban® Antimicrobial Protection: The only shade fabric with built-in protection against mold, mildew, and bacteria—crucial for humid environments.
  • GREENGUARD® Gold Certified: Ensures low chemical emissions for superior indoor air quality, safe for children and pets.
  • Superior Heat & UV Blockage: Blocks up to 99% of solar heat and UV rays, effectively retaining aircon temperature inside while reducing sun glare.
Italy’s Elitex Fabric — Exceptional Craftsmanship
Elitex is a globally trusted Italian brand, known for its exceptional craftsmanship and innovative fabric technology. Using advanced equipment and rigorous lab testing, Elitex guarantees consistent, high-performance fabrics that stand the test of time.
Our extensive range includes ScreenView, Blackout, Cross Stitch, and other specialty fabrics—each engineered with strict quality control and industry-leading standards. Experience the difference with Elitex—trusted by professionals worldwide.
